Section one

The scenery of the sea at dawn is always preserved in my memory. My hometown is associated with the sun and the wind of the sea. Every morning, I often go to the beach to watch the sunrise and wait for the boats to come ashore. In the morning sunlight, the sea is incredibly beautiful. The sun peeks out from behind the white clouds. The pale yellow sunlight shines down on the water, making the sea glitter. The waves crashing onto the shore create delightful sounds. The wind carries the scent of the sea, rustling through my hair. The white sandy beach is as smooth as a strip of silk blending into the blue of the sea. The sea has receded quite far compared to the previous evening.

Section Three

Every time I look at a photo of my homeland, I remember the beauty of nature there. That summer, it rained heavily, flooding the entire village. The farmers hurriedly went to the fields to check the rice with many worries. Finally, after many days of heavy rain, the sun rose high. The water in the fields also receded. In the evening, we children went out to fly kites. The wind blew gently, lifting the kites higher and higher until they were just a small dot in the distance. The rain washed away all the dirt from the earth and returned the sky to the people, clear and bright. White clouds floated in the sky.
